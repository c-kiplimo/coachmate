FAQs

To create a new coaching session click the Add session button. Fill in the required information and click "Save" to create the new session.

To edit your profile information, navigate to the settings page and click the settings icon. Make the necessary changes and click "Save" to update your profile.

To register as a coach or client, click on the Register button in the home page and fill out the registration form.

To record a payment, click on the Add payment button in the account summary page and fill out the details.

To add a client, click on the Add client button in the account summary page and fill out the details.

To add a contract, click on the Add contract button on the side nav and fill out the details.
