Key Features

Scheduling and Alerts:

The system offers a comprehensive scheduling tool that helps coaches and clients manage their time efficiently. It sends alerts for upcoming meetings and payments, ensuring that no important appointments are missed.

Feedback Management:

The system enables clients to provide feedback electronically, making it easier for coaches to track progress and improve their services.

Analytics and Reporting:

The system offers robust analytics and reporting features that provide coaches with detailed insights into their performance, enabling them to make data-driven decisions and improve their coaching services.

Payment Management:

The system provides a secure and convenient way for clients to manage their payments, eliminating the need for manual transactions and reducing the risk of errors.

Contract Management:

The system provides a digital platform for the signing of coaching contracts, which streamlines the process and reduces the risk of errors.
